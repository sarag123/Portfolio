Portfolio Projects

Brooklyn Public Library- Targeted Book Search

I collaborated with a team to improve the Brooklyn Public Library's website, making it easier for users to find books tailored to their interests. We identified key issues and developed intuitive interactions to enhance the online experience.

Department of Agriculture- RWD Redesign

I redesigned the USDA website with a fresh, modern look, using purpose-matching colors to enhance visual clarity. By applying card sorting, we simplified the design, optimizing topic categorization and improving usability through content reorganization.

Budget Savor Travel App- True Travel

A budget-friendly travel app was designed to help users save money while they travel. Extensive research led to the implementation of a budget calculator feature in the app. This tool assists users in saving both before and during their trips.
